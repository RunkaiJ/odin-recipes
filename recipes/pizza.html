Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish featuring a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese, customizable with endless toppings. Making pizza at home allows full control over flavor and texture.

This recipe keeps things simple while delivering a classic, satisfying result.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Desired toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Brush dough lightly with olive oil.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  5. Add mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place desired toppings on top.
  7. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  9. Garnish with basil and serve hot.
