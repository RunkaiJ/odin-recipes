Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. It’s hearty, filling, and ideal for family meals or gatherings

This recipe delivers deep flavor through slow-simmered tomato sauce and a generous blend of cheeses, creating a satisfying and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then brown the ground beef.
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Mix ricotta with egg and some Parmesan.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 40–45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest before slicing and serving.
