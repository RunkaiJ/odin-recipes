Ramen

Description

Ramen is a comforting Japanese noodle soup known for its rich broth and variety of toppings. Itâ€™s warm, flavorful, and perfect for quick meals or cozy evenings.

This recipe uses simple ingredients to create a savory broth while allowing flexibility for customization.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green onions
  • Eggs
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach or bok choy
  • Chili oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring broth to a gentle simmer.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Cook ramen noodles in the broth.
  4. Add mushrooms and greens until tender.
  5. Soft-boil eggs separately and slice.
  6. Pour ramen into bowls.
  7. Top with eggs and green onions.
  8. Add chili oil if desired and serve immediately.
